# Privacy Policy

**Effective July 1, 2026**

This Policy describes how Riskly, operated by Focuser LLC, handles your information — including demographics, activities, AI processing, subscriptions, and backups.

## 1. Who we are
Riskly (the "App") is operated by Focuser LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Riskly you agree to this Policy.

## 2. Information you provide
- **Demographics & lifestyle inputs:** If you choose to add them, we process details such as age, sex, and lifestyle answers to calculate your baseline estimate. These are optional and you can edit or delete them at any time.
- **Activities & answers:** The activities you log and the questions you answer (frequency, safety, experience, etc.) are used to generate risk estimates and scenarios.
- **Account information:** If you create an account, we process your email address to enable cloud backup and restore.

## 3. Information created by your use
- **Usage of AI features:** We track how many AI credits you have used and remaining, when they reset, and any credits you have purchased, so the App can enforce limits and top-ups.
- **Generated content:** Risk estimates, worst-case scenarios, and assessments produced for you are stored so you can view your history.

## 4. How AI processing works
Some features send the activity names and answers you provide to third-party AI model providers through a secure gateway in order to generate estimates, scenarios, and assessments. We do not send your email, precise identity, or contact details to these providers as part of that request.

AI output is generated automatically from generalized data and may be inaccurate or incomplete. It is provided for entertainment and educational purposes only and is not medical, legal, insurance, financial, or safety advice.

## 5. Subscriptions & purchases
Riskly offers auto-renewing subscriptions and one-time AI credit purchases. Payments, billing, and renewals are processed by the Apple App Store — we never receive or store your full payment card details.

We use a subscription management provider (RevenueCat) to verify your subscription status and entitlements. This involves processing a pseudonymous purchaser identifier and your transaction/receipt information from the App Store.

We may process the status of your trial, the plan you selected (weekly or monthly), renewal or expiry dates, and restore requests to give you access to paid features.

## 6. Data storage & backup
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, your data is also backed up to our secure cloud provider so you can restore it on another device.

You can delete all of your data at any time from **Settings → Delete all data**, which erases your activities, demographics, and saved logs and resets the App.

## 7. How we use information
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
- To calculate estimates and generate scenarios you request.
- To manage your AI credits, subscriptions, backups, and restores.
- To protect against fraud, abuse, and to comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

## 8. Sharing
We share information only with service providers who help us operate the App — for example, AI model providers, our cloud backup provider, the subscription management provider, and the App Store. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.

We may disclose information if required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the public.

## 9. Data retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete your data, it is removed from the App; backup copies are removed in the ordinary course of our provider's deletion cycles.

## 10. Children's privacy
Riskly is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 11. Your rights & choices
Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise many of these directly in the App, or by contacting us.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time in your App Store account settings.

## 12. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 13. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

## 14. Contact us
Questions about this Policy or your data? Contact Focuser LLC at [info@focuserllc.com](mailto:info@focuserllc.com).
