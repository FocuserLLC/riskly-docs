# Riskly Support

Need help with Riskly? We're here for you.

## Contact us

- **Email:** [info@focuserllc.com](mailto:info@focuserllc.com)
- **Response time:** We typically reply within 1–2 business days.

When you email us, it helps to include:

- The device you're using (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 18)
- The app version (find it in **Settings** inside the app)
- A short description of what happened, and a screenshot if you have one

## Common help topics

### Managing your subscription
Riskly offers auto-renewing weekly and monthly subscriptions. To view, change, or cancel your plan:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name at the top, then **Subscriptions**.
3. Select **Riskly** to change or cancel.

Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the current billing period. Deleting the app does **not** cancel your subscription.

### Restoring purchases
Reinstalled the app or switched devices? Open Riskly → **Settings → Restore Purchases** to restore your subscription and any credit packs tied to your Apple ID.

### AI credits
Riskly's AI features use credits. You get a free weekly allowance that refreshes automatically, and you can buy additional credit packs at any time. Purchased credits don't expire; free weekly credits don't roll over.

### Backup & restore
Your data is stored on your device. If you sign in, it's also backed up securely so you can restore it on another device. You can delete all your data anytime from **Settings → Delete all data**.

### Refunds
All purchases are handled by the Apple App Store. To request a refund, visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) and sign in with your Apple ID.
